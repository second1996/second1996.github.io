Why is Sorbiforce better than any other battery on the market?

Resource-independent manufacture

All components can be mined in the U.S., and production is independent of resource markets and foreign supplies. The Sorbiforce battery can potentially become a 100% locally manufactured U.S. product.

Zero environmental impact

No toxic products or metals are used in production. The main component of the battery is ultra-porous carbon. The raw material is agricultural residues such as straw. All battery elements can be easily disposed of and recycled.

Highest cost efficiency

The cost of 1 kWh is 1.8 times lower than the price of 1 kWh on the lithium-ion battery market. The technology is easily scalable and has the lowest CAPE.

Safe to use

The battery is resistant to mechanical damage, non-flammable, non-explosive, has no problem with overcharging, requires no cooling, and has no thermal loss. The bromine salt ZnBr2 in our battery is used in firefighting.
